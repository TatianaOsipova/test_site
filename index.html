Main stages of work?

The construction process is the detailed steps required to complete your construction project. This process can be broken down into five phases – planning/design, pre-construction, procurement, construction, and post-construction. Depending on the size and scope of the project, each phase has its own set of challenges.

Can we make a joint design project?

The aim of joint design is to obtain maximum strength for a given area of a bond. In designing joints specifically for adhesive bonding, the basic characteristics of adhesives must dictate the design of joints. Adhesive bonds act over surface areas and not a single point.
